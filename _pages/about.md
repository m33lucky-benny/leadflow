## Our Story

LeadFlow was born from a simple observation: **the best local businesses are often invisible online**.

We noticed that many successful local businesses — the ones with loyal customers, great reviews, and steady revenue — have little to no digital presence. They're not on Google Search. They have no website. They're missing from social media.

Yet they have **dozens or hundreds of happy customers** leaving glowing reviews on Google Maps.

## The Problem

These businesses are:
- ✅ **Proven** — Good reviews = real customers
- ✅ **Successful** — They're making money offline
- ❌ **Invisible** — No website, no SEO, no digital strategy
- ❌ **Losing customers** — People who search online never find them

## The Opportunity

For agencies, consultants, and service providers, this is a **goldmine**:

- **Pre-qualified leads** — Reviews prove they're legitimate
- **High intent** — They need help but don't know where to start
- **Less competition** — Other agencies target businesses WITH websites
- **Bigger deals** — They need everything: website, SEO, social media, ads

## What LeadFlow Does

LeadFlow uses Google Places data to find businesses that match this exact profile:

1. **4+ star rating** (quality businesses)
2. **20+ reviews** (proven track record)
3. **No website** (invisible online)

Then we give you their contact information so you can reach out with a compelling pitch:

> *"Hi, I found your business on Google — you have amazing reviews! But I couldn't find your website. You're losing customers who search online every day. Can we chat about fixing that?"*

## Who It's For

- **Web designers** — Find businesses that desperately need a website
- **SEO consultants** — Target businesses with zero search visibility
- **Marketing agencies** — Full digital transformation opportunities
- **Payment consultants** — Local retailers needing digital payment solutions
- **Any B2B service provider** targeting local businesses

## Our Mission

To connect great local businesses with the digital help they need — and help agencies find their next best clients.
